Our Story

Welcome, Illumineer

GTA Lorcana is the home for Disney Lorcana players across the Greater Toronto Area. Whether you're a seasoned Illumineer or just discovering the magic of glimmers and ink, you belong here.

We gather for casual play nights, competitive tournaments, booster drafts, and everything in between. Our community is friendly, welcoming, and built around a shared love of the game.

Most of our day-to-day chat, event coordination, and deck building discussion happens on our Discord — the best place to stay connected.
